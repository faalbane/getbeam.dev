Questions

What's included in Free?
You get 1 project workspace, 1 saved setup, unlimited tabs, all keyboard shortcuts, and search. It's the full app — just limited to a single project.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from the app's Settings panel whenever you want. Your license stays active until the end of the billing period, then reverts to Free.
Do I need separate AI subscriptions?
Yes — Beam organizes and launches AI agents, but you'll need your own API keys or subscriptions for Claude, Gemini, Codex, etc. Beam doesn't charge for AI usage.
What platforms are supported?
Beam runs on macOS (13+), Windows 10/11, and Linux. The same license works across all platforms.
